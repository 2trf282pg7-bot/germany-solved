Attempted to convert a job seeker visa to a Blue Card at Munich's KVR. The appointment wait was 12 weeks — booked through the official online system.

The officer cited insufficient salary documentation. Although my salary meets the Blue Card threshold (€43,800), they required a formal offer letter with explicit gross annual salary — my offer letter only stated monthly net. Was asked to return with updated documentation.

**Issue:** Offer letter format not accepted (net salary stated, not gross annual)
**Resolution:** Resubmitted 3 weeks later with corrected offer letter. Eventually approved.

**Tip:** Always get an offer letter stating gross annual salary explicitly.
